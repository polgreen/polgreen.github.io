Outside of the lab, I can usually be found near water. I both row and kayak (most rowers and kayakers will argue these are two completely different sports, but really the main difference is that kayakers travel facing forwards, rowers facing backwards).

I have rowed in The Boat Race twice, once for Cambridge in 2011 and once for Oxford in 2019. I have also represented England twice and won 4 national championships medals.

I took up kayaking in 2015 when I got bored of rowing. I have completed the 125 mile Devizes to Westminster race and raced for Great Britain at a World Cup.
