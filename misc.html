Outside of the lab, I can usually be found near water. I both row and kayak (for the uninitiated, the key difference is that kayakers face forwards, rowers face backwards).

I have rowed in The Boat Race twice, once for Oxford and once for Cambridge. I have also represented England twice, and won 4 national championships medals.

I took up kayaking in 2015 when I got bored of rowing. I have completed the 125 mile Devizes to Westminster race and raced for Great Britain at a World Cup. Now I'm in California, I am beginning to dabble in surfski.
