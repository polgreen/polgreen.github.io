I am a lecturer in the Laboratory for the Foundations of Computer Science at the University of Edinburgh where I hold a fellowship from the Royal Academic of Engineering. My research focuses on developing algorithms to automatically synthesize code, i.e., helping computers to program themselves, and proving that code is correct. Before doing a PhD, I was an electronics and software engineer at a design consultancy so I understand the pain points of programming, and my research strives to remove this pain from the day-to-day practice of software engineering.
